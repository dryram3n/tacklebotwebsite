Getting Started üöÄ

Welcome to TackleBot! Here's how to get started:

  1. Add TackleBot to your server using the "Invite Bot" button.
  2. Give TackleBot the right permissions - it needs permissions to send messages, use external emojis, add reactions, read message history, and potentially manage channels/messages/members if you plan to use moderation features.

  4. View help anytime with /help or specific guides like /help fishing

Note: TackleBot works in both server channels and direct messages (DMs).

Fishing System Guide üé£

Fishing is the core of TackleBot! Here's everything you need to know:

üìä Rarity System

Fish and items come in different rarities, each with its own value and catch probability:

  • Junk ‚ö´ - Worthless items, reduced by luck.
  • Common üü§ - Basic fish, low value.
  • Uncommon üü¢ - Better value.
  • Rare üîµ - Valuable fish.
  • Legendary üü° - Very valuable, hard to find.
  • Mythic üü£ - Extremely rare and valuable.
  • Chimerical üåü - The rarest fish of all.
  • Special Fish ‚ú® - Rare variants (e.g., Shimmering, Divine) with boosted stats and significantly higher value.
  • Seasonal Fish üçÇ - Appear only during specific real-world seasons.
  • Event Fish üéâ - Limited-time fish available during special events.
  • Treasure Chests üì¶ - Contain random coin amounts. Tiers include Wooden, Metal, Golden, Ancient, Mysterious.

Higher rarity fish are more valuable and give more XP, but are harder to catch! Luck upgrades help find better fish.

üìà Leveling & Experience

As you fish, you'll gain experience (XP) and level up:

  • XP is gained primarily from catching fish (rarer fish give more XP).
  • Completing challenges and achievements also grants XP.
  • Higher levels unlock:
    • Better base fishing rates and stats.
    • New shop upgrades and research options.
    • Access to new fishing locations.
    • Higher minimum values for Voter Chests.
  • Check your level, XP, and progress with /fish stats.
üìñ Fish Collection & Index

TackleBot keeps track of all the fish species you've discovered:

  • Access your collection via the "Fish Index" button on the /fish stats page.
  • Organized by rarity and location.
  • Shows your progress toward collecting every species.
  • View images, lore, stats, and records (biggest/smallest) for each fish.
  • Discover special seasonal and event variants.
  • Some fish are only available in specific locations or seasons.
üìè Fish Size Categories

Fish you catch come in different sizes, affecting their name and sometimes value:

  • Sizes are based on weight and length compared to the species' average.
  • Categories include: Baby, Small, Big, Adult, Massive, Colossal.
  • The size category is added to the fish's name (e.g., "Massive Common Carp").
  • Check your biggest and smallest catches in the Fish Index!

Fishing Business üö¢

Become a fishing tycoon by starting your own Fishing Business! Generate passive income to fuel your adventures.

üìà Managing Your Business
  • Access: Manage your business via the "My Businesses" button on /fish stats or after using /fish cast.
  • Purchase: Start your first business (e.g., Fishing Fleet, Market Stall) for a significant coin investment.
  • Passive Income: Businesses generate income automatically over time based on their level and specific upgrades (like Boats, Nets, Engines, Workers for the Fleet).
  • Collect Income: Manually collect your earnings using the "Collect Income" button in the business management view.
  • Upgrades: Invest more coins to upgrade your business components (e.g., buy more Boats, improve Nets) to increase income generation rate and capacity. Access via the "Manage Upgrades" button.
  • Multiple Businesses: You can own and manage different types of businesses simultaneously (e.g., Fleet, Market).

Strategy: Reinvesting your profits into business upgrades is key to maximizing your passive income stream!

Running a Business

Invest your hard-earned cash into businesses to generate passive income. Different businesses offer varying returns.

Use /business buy [business_name] to purchase a business and /business list to see available options and your current holdings.

**Passive Income:** Your businesses generate income automatically over time. **This income is automatically claimed and added to your balance every minute**, even if you're not actively playing!

Upgrade your businesses using /business upgrade [business_name] to increase their profitability.

Moderation Guide üõ°Ô∏è

TackleBot includes useful moderation features for your server (require appropriate Discord permissions or Bot Moderator role):

User Management

  • /kick [user] [reason] - Remove user (Requires Kick Members perm or Mod role)
  • /ban [user] [reason] [days] - Ban user, optionally delete messages (Requires Ban Members perm or Mod role)
  • /clear [amount] - Delete 1-100 recent messages (Requires Manage Messages perm or Mod role)

Bot Moderators

  • /moderator add [user] - Grant a user permission to use TackleBot's mod commands (`/kick`, `/ban`, `/clear`, `/deletechannel`).
  • /moderator remove [user] - Remove bot moderator permissions.
  • Requires: Server Owner permission.

Channel Management

  • /createchannel [name] type:[text/voice] [private] [member1...] - Create text/voice channels, optionally private. (Requires Manage Channels perm)
  • /deletechannel - Delete the channel the command is used in. (Requires Manage Channels perm, Mod role, or being the channel creator)
  • Limit: Users have limits on channels they can create.

Server Logging

  • /log setup [channel] - Set a channel to log server events (message deletes, member joins/leaves).
  • /log disable - Disable server event logging.
  • Requires: Administrator permission.

Note: Bot Moderators (`/moderator add`) only get permission for TackleBot's commands, not full Discord permissions.

The core of TackleBot! Here's the lowdown:
