1. Overview

TackleBot is the ultimate fishing RPG for Discord. With over 15 unique locations, hundreds of fish species, and a dynamic economy, it offers an immersive experience right in your server. Whether you're a casual angler or a competitive tycoon, there's something for everyone.

Start your journey with a basic rod and work your way up to legendary equipment. Explore the depths of the ocean, mysterious cosmic seas, and everything in between.

2. Core Fishing Mechanics

Fishing

To start fishing, simply use the /fish command. You'll catch fish based on your current location and bait. Different biomes have different fish, so exploration is key.

Equipment

Upgrade your rod to catch rarer and heavier fish. Better rods also allow you to fish in more dangerous locations without breaking your line.

Bait

Use specific baits to target legendary species or increase your catch rate. Baits can be bought in the shop or crafted.

3. Economy & Currency

Gold is the primary currency in TackleBot. Earn it by selling your catch at the market using /sell. The market prices fluctuate, so time your sales for maximum profit.

Use Gold to buy upgrades, boats, and consumables in the /shop. Manage your finances wisely to become the wealthiest fisher in the server.

4. Business Empire

Once you've established yourself, you can start your own business empire. Buy Aquariums to display your rare catches and generate passive income over time.

Invest in Fishing Fleets to automate your income. Hire captains and crew to fish for you while you're away.

5. Pet System

Hatch pets from eggs found while fishing. Pets are loyal companions that level up as you fish.

Pet Bonuses

  • Catch Rate: Increases the chance of catching fish.
  • Gold Multiplier: Increases the gold earned from selling fish.
  • Treasure Hunter: Increases the chance of finding rare items.

Collect them all and find the perfect companion for your playstyle!

6. Social & Competitive

Leaderboards

Compete in global leaderboards for the biggest catch, most gold, or highest level. Show off your achievements to the world.

Tournaments

Join regular fishing tournaments to win exclusive rewards, badges, and massive gold prizes.

Guilds

Form fishing guilds with your friends. Work together to unlock guild-wide bonuses and compete against other guilds.

7. Progression Systems

Level up your fishing skill to unlock new areas and equipment. Each level brings new challenges and rewards.

Complete Achievements to earn badges and special items. The journey from a novice angler to a fishing master is filled with discoveries.
